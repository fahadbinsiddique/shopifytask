Why is short-form video so
popular?

Video content in the short form is not new. In actuality, the 2012-released short-form
video app Vine was a pioneer of the genre.


But as tech giants like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube launched short-form video
features of their own, what had initially been a niche platform grew into something
much larger.
